About Me

My name is Mikhail Metrikin and this is my simple portfolio page. I move to the United States from Moscow, Russia when I was very young and settled in California. I have been in the San Francisco/Bay Area much of my life and graduated from the University of California, Berkeley. After working for a few years as a consultant for Sales and Marketing strategies to many tech firms, I decided to start teaching myself to code, seeing it as the central heart of all competitive companies.

Since then I have learned a number of frameworks and languages, including C++, Java, Python, Javascript, HTML/CSS and more. I applied my newly gained knowledge initially working as an intern Automated Testing Engineer developing Selenium and Java automation tests in Python, and JUnit respectively. Currently I am improving my front-end knowledge, and taking a course in Data Structures.
